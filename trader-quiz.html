Why Trading Psychology Matters

Most traders focus only on strategy, indicators, and market timing. However, long-term performance is often determined by psychological stability and risk management discipline rather than technical tools alone.

Understanding how you react to volatility, drawdowns, and uncertainty can reveal structural strengths and weaknesses in your trading behavior. Emotional overreaction, revenge trading, and poor risk allocation are among the most common causes of capital erosion.
